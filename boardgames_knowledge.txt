# Gloomhaven
Gloomhaven is a cooperative game of battling monsters and advancing a player's own individual goals. The game is meant for 1 to 4 players and takes approximately 30 minutes per player.

Players will take on the role of a wandering mercenary with their own special set of skills and their own reasons for traveling to this remote corner of the world.

# Catan
Catan, previously known as The Settlers of Catan, is a multiplayer board game designed by Klaus Teuber. Players take on the roles of settlers, each attempting to build and develop holdings while trading and acquiring resources.

The game is won by reaching a certain number of victory points, which are earned by building settlements, cities, and roads, as well as by holding certain cards.

# Ticket to Ride
Ticket to Ride is a railway-themed German-style board game designed by Alan R. Moon. The object of the game is to score the highest number of points.

Players collect cards of various types of train cars that enable them to claim railway routes connecting cities in various countries around the world.

# Pandemic
Pandemic is a cooperative board game designed by Matt Leacock. The game accommodates two to four players and requires them to work together to treat infections while researching cures.

As members of an elite disease control team, players must keep four deadly diseases at bay while discovering their cures.
